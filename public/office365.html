Office 365 Security

An detailed audit and action plan to protect your investment

Why is security important

Microsoft Office 365 is shared security responsiblity model. This means the customer of the product owns some responsiblity for ensuring the security of the data.

What will you get with this audit?

A detailed PDF summary of findings and ways to remidate them. Findings will be linked to NIST standards. The audit will cover basic security misconfigurations and areas often missed to help ensure the security of your cloud data, delivery of email, and protection of user accounts

Get Better Protection Against:

  • Ransomware and other viruses
  • Phising emails designed to trick you into parting with your money
  • Emails being marked as junk by customers you are working with
  • Passwords being guessed
  • Internal mistakes or deleted files
